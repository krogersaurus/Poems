My mind wandered
Right to your front door
A gentle push is all it takes to swing it open
Sunlight and birdsong slip between the blinds and bounce off the walls
It's empty here now, peaceful
Your frames still hang on the walls, your TV is off like always.
It's still yours, but you don't live here anymore
There is nothing more to do here. No objective. 
I think I'll stay and listen to the silence 
I think I'll linger a little longer

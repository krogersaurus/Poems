A heavy heart follows me everywhere I go
It sits next to me, squeezing into gaps, deficiencies and voids,
like words trying to fill a cavernous and hungry silence.
A heavy heart binds like chains and pulls you into the ground. In your shoes, it wears spots that you know, that you can trace with your fingers. You can remember rainy nights, wet socks. 
A heavy heart pumps slowly, laboriously. 
A heavy heart feels eyes and hears tongues speaking a language that everyone else seems to understand.  
A heavy heart is patient;
...
A heavy heart looks but does not touch
A heavy heart is not easily moved
